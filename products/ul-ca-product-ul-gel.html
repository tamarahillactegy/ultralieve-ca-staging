About Ultralieve

Ultralieve is designed to relieve pain by supporting the healing process to aid tissue repair. Whether it’s gardening, cycling, looking after the kids, going for a long walk or a physically demanding job, the ability to move with freedom is vital to your everyday life.

Ultralieve uses very high frequency sound vibrations (so high they cannot be felt) to stimulate tissue up to 5cm beneath the skin’s surface. Non-thermal ultrasound, as used by Ultralieve, speeds up the healing process by allowing the body to better transport proteins that are the essential building blocks to generate new tissue. For the example of a ligament tear, the application of ultrasound can help speed up production of collagen.

Ultimately, Ultralieve supports your body’s natural healing process so you can get back to your best.
